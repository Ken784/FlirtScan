# FlirtScan Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 22, 2025**

Thank you for using FlirtScan (referred to as "this app" or "we"). We take your privacy seriously, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## 1. Information Collection

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Conversation Screenshots**: When you upload conversation screenshots for analysis, we collect the image content you select
- **Analysis Results**: Analysis results are stored locally on your device for your viewing only

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, application version
- **Usage Data**: Application usage patterns, feature usage frequency, error logs
- **Advertising-Related Data**: To provide advertising services, we may collect advertising interaction data (using non-personalized ad mode)

### 1.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Services
This app uses the following third-party services, which may collect related information:
- **Firebase** (Google): Used for application backend services and anonymous analytics
- **OpenAI API**: Used for AI analysis of conversation content
- **Google AdMob**: Used for displaying advertisements
- **Google ML Kit**: Used for image text recognition (OCR)

## 2. How We Use Information

### 2.1 Conversation Analysis Processing
- Your uploaded conversation screenshots are processed through the following steps:
  1. **Local OCR Processing**: Uses Google ML Kit on your device for text recognition
  2. **AI Analysis**: Recognized text content is sent to our backend service (Firebase Cloud Functions) and analyzed by OpenAI API
  3. **Temporary Processing**: Conversation content (including names) is temporarily stored in server memory only during analysis processing and **is not persistently stored**
  4. **After Analysis Completion**: After processing is complete, the original conversation text is cleared from server memory

### 2.2 Local Storage
- Analysis results are stored locally on your device (using encrypted storage)
- You can delete these records at any time within the application
- We **do not** store your conversation content or analysis results on our servers

### 2.3 Anonymous Analytics
We may collect the following anonymous analytics data (which does not include personally identifiable information):
- Application download and usage frequency
- Analysis feature usage count
- Average conversation length
- Advertising interaction statistics
- Error reports (for improving application stability)

### 2.4 Advertising Services
- We use Google AdMob to provide rewarded advertisements (Rewarded Ads)
- Default mode is **Non-Personalized Ads (NPA)**, which does not target ads based on your personal information
- Advertising interaction data is only used for ad redemption verification and not for other purposes

We may disclose your information in the following circumstances:
- To comply with laws, regulations, court orders, or government requests
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To prevent fraud or other illegal activities

### 3.3 What We Do Not Do
- **Do not** sell your conversation content or personal information to third parties
- **Do not** use your conversation content to train AI models (OpenAI API usage follows their privacy policy)
- **Do not** persistently store your conversation content on our servers

## 4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your information:
- **Encrypted Transmission**: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- **Local Encrypted Storage**: Analysis results are stored encrypted on your device
- **Minimal Data Collection**: We only collect information necessary to provide services
- **Temporary Processing**: Conversation content is only temporarily stored during analysis and immediately cleared after processing
- **Firebase App Check**: Application verification is enabled to restrict unauthorized access

## 5. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR, CCPA), you have the following rights:

### 5.1 Right to Access
- You can view analysis records stored locally in the application at any time

### 5.2 Right to Deletion
- You can delete analysis records at any time within the application
- To completely delete all data, please contact us using the methods below

### 5.3 Right to Withdraw Consent
- You can stop using this app at any time
- You can manage advertising tracking preferences through device settings (iOS ATT, Google UMP)

### 5.4 Right to Data Portability
- You can export analysis results through the application's share feature

### 5.5 Right to Complain
- If you believe we have violated privacy regulations, you have the right to file a complaint with relevant regulatory authorities

## 6. Children's Privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will immediately delete such information.
